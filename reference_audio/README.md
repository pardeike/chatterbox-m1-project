# Voice Reference Audio Folder

Place your voice reference files here for voice cloning.

## Supported Formats
- .wav (recommended)
- .mp3

## Guidelines for Best Results

### Duration
- **Optimal**: 10-30 seconds
- **Minimum**: 3 seconds
- **Maximum**: 60 seconds

### Content
- Single speaker only
- Natural conversational speech
- Avoid background music or noise
- Clear pronunciation

### Quality
- High-quality recording (44.1kHz or 48kHz recommended)
- Minimal background noise
- Good microphone quality
- Consistent volume level

### Speaking Style
- Natural pace (not too fast or slow)
- Clear articulation
- Variety of sounds (vowels and consonants)
- Avoid monotone delivery

## Example Texts to Record
- "Hello, this is a sample of my voice for cloning purposes."
- "The weather today is beautiful and I'm excited to test this technology."
- "I hope this voice cloning demonstration works well on my MacBook Air."

## How to Use
1. Record your voice sample using QuickTime Player, Voice Memos, or any audio app
2. Save as .wav file in this folder
3. Run: `./run_voice_cloning.sh`

The voice cloning system will automatically detect and use any audio files in this folder.
